The structure of the files:
VARIABLE_SCENARIO_PERIOD

### Variables ###
bio01 = Annual Mean Temperature
bio12 = Annual Mean Precipitation

### Scenarios ###
126 = SSP1 - 2.6
585 = SSP5 - 8.5

### Periods ###
1981_2010 = 1981-2010*
2011_2040 = 2011-2040
2041_2070 = 2041-2070
2071_2100 = 2071-2100

*There are no scenarios for this period, so the two scenarios are the same file with different names.
